What's XDRS ?

Xdrs is a private community made for doctors only that allows them to share knowledge and experience through posting videos, updates, journals and more. Not only that it provides them with a community, but also, courses in their speciality from begginer to advanced level, specialized medical videos library, ebooks library and a marketplace. all of these in one pocket sized platform as a mobile application.
